Tom Goodyear

Chief Operating Officer

Thoroughly excited for the next stage of the journey with Siena – we’re building a great team, sourcing top talent for our clients and helping exceptional candidates find their next stage in their careers

Tom has 13 years of experience working within technology and change directly with the C-suite and their leadership teams across Investment, Retail and Challenger Banks, Asset and Investment Managers, Private Equity, Fintech, Insurance and Big Tech. Former Partner of a Management Consultancy where he established a financial services practice across UK & Ireland.

Prior to this Tom, was a part of the leadership team at Harvey Nash within the Finance & Banking division, responsible for building a greenfield core technology practice.
